The Wilfrid Laurier University Korean Students' Association (WLU KSA) is a community dedicated to celebrating and sharing Korean culture with the Laurier student body and beyond. Our organization provides a welcoming space for students of Korean heritage and those interested in Korean culture to connect, learn, and engage.

Through cultural events, social gatherings, and educational workshops, we aim to foster a sense of unity and cultural pride, while also building bridges with the wider Laurier community.
